This is my homepage. I will have a navigation bar that will link to the Recipe Spinner, my About page, and pages for Main dishes, Vegetables, Side dishes, and Desserts. I will have a search bar as well. Below the navigation bar and search bar, I will have two rows of 3 separate recipe descriptions. Each recipe will have a completed picture next to a short description of the recipe. When you click on the picture it will lead to that recipe's page. The first row will be recently added recipes, and the second row will be most popular recipes.
