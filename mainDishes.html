This is my Main Dishes page. I haven't decided if I will have some sort of selector to narrow down the display of recipes based on category of protein (Beef, Poultry, Pork, Seafood, or Vegetarian). Either way, the page will have a display much like the home page. I will have a navigation bar identical to the home page and a search bar as well. Below the navigation bar and search bar, I will have two rows of 3 separate recipe descriptions. Each recipe will have a completed picture next to a short description of the recipe. When you click on the picture it will lead to that recipe's page. The first row will be recently added recipes, and the second row will be most popular recipes.
