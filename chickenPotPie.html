Chicken Pot Pie

  • needs: 2 9-inch pie tins, 1 large pot, 1 sauce pan
  • prep time: 30 minutes
  • cook time: 60 minutes
  • serves: 2 pies

ingredients

  • 2 lbs. boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cubed
  • 5 c. frozen mixed vegetables
  • 1/2 c. butter
  • 1 c. onion, chopped
  • 1 c. all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp.: parseley, thyme, rosemary, black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • 2 tsp. chicken buillon
  • 2 c. milk
  • 2 packages of 2 9-inch pie crusts

directions

  • 1. Bring a large pot filled halfway with water to a boil. Add chicken, cover, and boil for 10 minutes. Drain. Combine with vegetables.
  • 2. Preheat oven to 425. Press pie dough into tins.
  • 3. In saucepan, melt butter over medium-low heat. Saute onions until soft and translucent. Stir in flour, spices, and buillon. Cook for 2-3 minutes, stirring frequently.
  • 4. Slowly stir in milk, whisking constantly. Increase heat and whisk until pudding consistency. Remove from heat.
  • 5. Mix together chicken/veggies and onions/sauce. Fill each tin with half the mixture. Cover with second pie crust. Trim and pinch shut. Slice 5 slits in each pie to release steam.
  • 6. Bake for 35 minutes until golden brown. If edges brown too quickly, cover with aluminum foil. Let stand 15 minutes before serving.
