This is my Recipe Spinner Page. I will have a navigation bar identical to the home page and a search bar as well. Below the navigation bar and search bar, there will be a short form that asks you to select a type of dish from a menu. In the case of Main dish, it will also ask you to select from Beef, Poultry, Pork, Seafood, or Vegetarian. Then you press the "spin" button and it redirects you to a randomly selected page from the category you chose.
